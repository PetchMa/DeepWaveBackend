DeepGalaxy

Research. Test. Run.

What Does It Do?

Deep LearningDeep Galaxy is a deep learning model aimed to enhance astronomical images through deep super sampling and super resolution. Researchers in astrophysics can often times be hindered by poor quality or shortly exposed images.

This one MAJOR problem preventing us from learning more about the fundementals of our universe.

Deep Galaxy uses AI to create realistic and high quality images from lower sampled inputs. As simple as it gets.

Try It Yourself

Take an image from your choosing (MUST BE 25X25) and feed it into the neural network. The model will process your upload by forward passing the image through a pretrained neural network located in a server.
