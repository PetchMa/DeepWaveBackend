Welcome

DeepWave is a AI development project built to apply Deep Learning to solve real world problems. DeepWave encompasses various different branches of research in Deep Learning but is focused on using these tools to process wave type data. This includes, EEG waves, and Electromagnetic waves (image processing).

EEG Noise

One of the biggest setbacks in the development of Brain Computer Interfaces is noise. This noise caused by body movement and electronics interfere with the accuracy of these non-invasive devices. What if we can unlock that potential by using AI to clean the noise?

DeepNoise Clean

Satellite Imaging

Our current modern technology can help us humans peer deep into galatic space millions of light years away. Yet the images we take can often lack detail. Can we train a AI model to upsample small images and insert detail in the images we take.

DeepGalaxy
